About FIELLDAY

FIELLDAY is a brand from Cherkasy specializing in the production of agricultural equipment, particularly harvesters. Their machinery ensures high productivity and quality crop harvesting, helping to increase business profitability and significantly improve the efficiency of agricultural work.

"Our factory is a place where every day we work on creating innovative solutions that will help increase productivity and efficiency in agriculture. Our goal is to help agriculture become more efficient and profitable. We strive to create equipment that optimizes workflow processes and increases productivity."
