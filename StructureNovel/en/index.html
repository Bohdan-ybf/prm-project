Pi Makina is the first and only Turkish manufacturer of a diverse range of construction and work equipment, such as concrete plants, asphalt pavers, concrete mixers, graders, loaders, excavator-loaders, tower cranes, and more.

The company is a major manufacturer and plays a significant role in national and global industries. Pi Makina employs over 1000 staff and has production facilities covering 800,000 square meters. Over half a century of operation, the company has become the preferred choice for both domestic and international businesses. This success allowed Pi Makina to sell over 10,000 units of equipment and machinery.

Our main mission is to produce innovative, environmentally friendly, energy-efficient, durable products at fair prices, as well as a customer-oriented approach in post-production support services.
